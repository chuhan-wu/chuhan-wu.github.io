Gold Rush

Medium: Ableton Live
“Gold Rush” is a movement of first generation of immigrated Chinese people came to San Francisco to discover gold. As the resource of gold has been fully discovered, these group of people started working for American industries to build up railways, which were the first railway of America that support their future social and industrial development. Unfortunately, this group of people and their contribution were gradually forgot by this land. “Gold Rush” is for memorizing the initial generation of Chinese Immigrants who contributed to this land.
