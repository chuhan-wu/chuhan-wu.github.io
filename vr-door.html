Doors

Medium: Unity, Oculus
DOORS is a VR project, which allows the audience to experience the situation of women being gender discriminated by people in society, especially in many professional fields such as film production, technical company, science research, etc. The employment rate of women in such field is significantly lower than males, which causes a false interpretation of women’s abilities in these areas and lead to a fear of women who have the passion of such job at the same time.

Description:

Women represented significant low rate of employment in many working areas. By demonstrating four doors that mostly represent this gender bias, we offer our user the free choice to experience the scene they want. We Include four doors of Hollywood, Tech Company, Chemical Company,

 

and Michelin Restaurant, which female only takes a very small portion. We involved real recording of how women are usually being judged by others in this project for better experience. In each room, we also include data information to demonstrate the reality, so that our audience may understand the situation clearly.
